About

Rit-Beulah Engineering Services Limited (RC. 382490) fully owned Nigerian registered private Limited Liability company, ISO Certified by the the Standards Organization of Nigeria to operate in Oil and Gas Sector. Our services include; Mudlogging, Measurement while Drilling, Pipeline Construction & Maintenance, Wellflow Management, Well Completion Services, Slickline Services, Procurement of Engineering Equipment: OCTG, Valves, Flanges, Pumps, Compressors, Turnkeys, Manpower Supply Services. The company is principally involved in the provision of support and maintenance services for companies operating in the oil and gas industry and related petrochemical sectors.
