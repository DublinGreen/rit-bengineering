About

Rit-Beulah Engineering Services Ltd was incorporated in May 2000, under the Companies and Allied Matters Act 1990. Its headquarters is in Lagos, Nigeria. The company is principally involved in the provision of support and maintenance services for companies operating in the oil and gas industry and related petrochemical sectors.

Early Production Facilities (EPF)

  1. Home
  2. Early Production Facilities (EPF)
Rit-Beulah provides scalable, modular EPF solutions to fast-track field production and early monetization with minimal lead time and high operational flexibility.

Our EPF Capabilities

  • Engineering, Procurement, Construction & Installation (EPCI) of EPF units
  • Inlet manifolds, test & production separators
  • Surge vessels, oil dehydrators
  • Gas treatment & flare systems
  • Produced water treatment (induced gas flotation, media polishers)
  • Storage tanks & loading gantries
  • Pumps, compressors, metering systems
  • Modular skid design for onshore, swamp, and offshore deployment

Why Choose Our EPF Solutions

  • Rapid mobilization: Ready for field deployment within 10–12 weeks
  • Scalable & modular: Tailor configurations to specific field requirements
  • Cost-effective: Optimized for early cash flow, prior to permanent facility build-out
  • Versatile terrain support: Ideal for onshore, swamp, coastal and offshore environments

Strategically located with full logistics and personnel support. Enables efficient equipment mobilization and rapid execution capabilities.
